### [1] [Second-Order Bilevel Optimization with Accelerated Convergence Rates](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06431)
*Sheng Yang,Chengchang Liu,Lesi Chen,John C. S. Lui*

Main category: math.OC

TL;DR: 本文针对非凸-强凸双层优化问题，提出了全二阶双层近似方法（FSBA）及其惰性变体（LFSBA），分别实现了$\tilde{\mathcal{O}}(ε^{-1.5})$的迭代复杂度和更好的计算复杂度，并将惰性思想推广到极小极大优化中提出LMCN方法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有双层优化方法多依赖一阶信息，收敛速度受限；二阶方法虽潜力更大但计算成本高昂。本文旨在探索二阶方法能否加速非凸-强凸双层优化的收敛，并解决其计算开销问题。

Method: 提出全二阶双层近似方法（FSBA），利用超目标函数的二阶信息进行优化；为降低计算成本，提出惰性变体LFSBA，在多次迭代中复用二阶信息；类似思想推广到非凸-强凹极小极大优化，提出惰性极小极大三次正则化牛顿方法（LMCN）。

Result: FSBA达到$\tilde{\mathcal{O}}(ε^{-1.5})$的迭代复杂度找到$(ε, \mathcal{O}(\sqrtε))$二阶稳定点；LFSBA计算复杂度比FSBA降低$\sqrt{d}$倍；LMCN相比现有二阶方法具有更优计算复杂度。

Conclusion: 二阶方法在双层优化中可实现比一阶方法更快的收敛速度，通过惰性策略可有效降低二阶方法的计算成本，相关思想可推广至极小极大优化。

Abstract: This paper studies second-order methods for nonconvex-strongly-convex bilevel optimization. We propose a novel fully second-order bilevel approximation method (FSBA) that achieves an iteration complexity of $\tilde{\mathcal{O}}(ε^{-1.5})$ for finding the $(ε, \mathcal{O}(\sqrtε))$ second-order stationary point of the hyper-objective function. Our results demonstrate that second-order methods can achieve an accelerated convergence rate than first-order methods in bilevel optimization. To address the heavy computational cost associated with the second-order oracle, we introduce a lazy variant of FSBA, called LFSBA, which reuses second-order information across several iterations. We prove that LFSBA exhibits better computational complexity than FSBA by a factor of $\sqrt{d}$, where $d$ is the dimension of the problem. We also apply a similar idea to nonconvex strongly-concave minimax optimization and propose the lazy minimax cubic-regularized Newton (LMCN) method with better computational complexity compared to existing second-order methods.

### [2] [Stochastic Non-Smooth Non-Convex Optimization with Decision-Dependent Distributions](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06549)
*Chengchang Liu,Zongqi Wan,Haishan Ye,John C. S. Lui*

Main category: math.OC

TL;DR: 本文提出了决策依赖分布下的随机零阶优化方法，在非光滑非凸、光滑和Hessian-Lipschitz三种目标设定下，分别建立了显式的收敛保证和最优的样本复杂度（SZO复杂度分别为O(d²δ⁻³ε⁻³)、O(d²ε⁻⁶)和O(d²ε⁻⁹/²)），并且在Hessian-Lipschitz情形下将ε的依赖关系提升了ε^{-1/2}。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现实中的许多优化问题（如在线广告投放、个性化推荐）存在决策依赖分布（decision-dependent distributions），即采样分布随当前决策变化而变化，传统零阶优化理论无法直接适用，因此需要建立新的理论框架和收敛保证。

Method: 采用Goldstein稳定点概念作为非凸非光滑情形的收敛目标，利用随机零阶梯度估计技术，在三种不同光滑性假设下（非光滑非凸、光滑、Hessian-Lipschitz）分别推导了复杂度上界，每轮迭代仅需单次SZO反馈。

Result: 1) 非光滑非凸：SZO复杂度 O(d²δ⁻³ε⁻³)，达到Goldstein稳定点；2) 光滑情形：O(d²ε⁻⁶)；3) Hessian-Lipschitz情形：O(d²ε⁻⁹/²)，相比已有最好结果在ε上提升了ε^{-1/2}。

Conclusion: 本文系统建立了决策依赖分布下零阶优化的收敛理论，首次为非光滑非凸情形提供了显式复杂度保证，并在Hessian-Lipschitz条件下改进了现有最优结果，仅需单次函数值反馈。

Abstract: We study stochastic zeroth-order optimization with decision-dependent distributions, where the sampling law depends on the current decision and only noisy function values are available. For the non-smooth non-convex setting, we establish an explicit convergence guarantee for finding a $(δ,ε)$-Goldstein stationary point with stochastic zeroth-order oracle (SZO) complexity of $\mathcal{O}(d^2δ^{-3}ε^{-3})$. In addition, we show that the above complexity can be achieved with single SZO feedback per iteration. We further extend the analysis to smooth and Hessian-Lipschitz objectives, obtaining complexities $\mathcal{O}(d^2ε^{-6})$ and $\mathcal{O}(d^2ε^{-9/2})$, respectively. In the Hessian-Lipschitz case, this improves the best-known dependence on $ε$ for decision-dependent zeroth-order methods by a factor of $ε^{-1/2}$.

### [3] [MTRBO: Multiple trust-region based Bayesian optimization](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06618)
*Sourav Das,Debjani Chakraborty,Pabitra Mitra*

Main category: math.OC

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种多信任域贝叶斯优化方法(MTRBO)，通过同时利用一个信任域进行局部利用（靠近当前最优解）和另一个信任域进行全局探索（最不确定区域），解决了高维黑盒函数优化中搜索空间指数增长、目标函数异质性和低采样预算的问题，并在理论上证明了全局收敛性，实验表明在多种非凸高维测试函数和投资组合优化问题上优于现有方法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 标准贝叶斯优化在高维问题中效率低下，主要原因包括：搜索空间随维度指数增长、目标函数存在异质性（不同区域变化剧烈程度不同）、以及采样预算有限（函数评估成本高）。现有方法难以同时兼顾局部利用和全局探索，需要一种更高效的信任域策略来平衡二者。

Method: 提出多信任域贝叶斯优化(MTRBO)，基于高斯过程作为目标函数的先验，同时维护两个信任域：一个信任域围绕当前最有希望的解决方案进行局部利用（基于后验均值），另一个信任域在搜索空间中最不确定的区域进行探索（基于后验方差）。通过自适应调整信任域的大小和位置，平衡探索与利用。同时建立了理论上的全局收敛性证明。

Result: 在多种非凸和高维测试函数上，MTRBO相比于其他最先进的基于信任域的贝叶斯优化算法，在给定的采样预算内取得了更优的解质量。在投资组合优化这一实际应用场景中也验证了其有效性。

Conclusion: MTRBO通过多信任域机制有效解决了高维贝叶斯优化中的探索-利用平衡问题，具有理论收敛性保证和实际应用价值，尤其在函数评估成本高昂的场景下表现出更好的样本效率。

Abstract: Bayesian Optimization (BO) is a popular framework for optimizing black-box functions. Despite its effectiveness, BO is often inefficient for high-dimensional problems due to the exponential growth of the search space, heterogeneity of the objective function, and low sampling budget. To overcome these issues, this work proposes a multiple trust region-based Bayesian optimization technique(MTRBO). A trust region is a localized region within which an optimization model is trusted to approximate the objective function accurately. Assuming a Gaussian process (GP) as a prior belief about the objective function and based on the posterior mean and variance functions, the method adaptively exploits near the promising current solution inside a trust region. Also explores the most uncertain region in the search space inside another trust region. The theoretical global convergence property of the proposed method is established. Then the work is benchmarked against other state-of-the-art trust-region-based Bayesian optimization algorithms, demonstrating superior performance on a variety of non-convex and high-dimensional test functions. The proposed method outperforms others in terms of solution quality within the sampling budget (the number of function evaluations). The proposed method is applied to the portfolio optimization problem to verify its applicability in real-world scenarios.

</details>
