### [1] [Symbolic Discovery of Iterative Algorithms: A Continuous Latent Space Bayesian Optimization Framework](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.01552)
*Tongjia Liu,Ilias Mitrai*

Main category: math.OC

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种利用潜空间贝叶斯优化自动发现迭代优化算法的框架，通过变分自编码器将离散的更新函数空间映射到连续空间进行搜索，能够在无需预设函数形式的情况下，以较低计算成本发现新的符号形式的更新函数。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有优化算法设计通常依赖人工经验和数学推导，效率低下且受限于预设的函数形式；自动化发现算法可减少人工干预，但目前方法计算成本高。本文旨在开发一种高效、无需预设函数形式的自动算法发现方法。

Method: 将算法发现任务建模为离散优化问题；使用变分自编码器学习离散更新函数空间的连续表示；在连续潜空间中利用贝叶斯优化搜索新的更新函数。

Result: 在两种案例研究中，该方法能够以符号形式发现新的更新函数，无需任何函数形式假设；计算时间低于现有的数学规划方法。

Conclusion: 所提出的潜空间贝叶斯优化框架为自动发现迭代优化算法提供了一种高效且灵活的途径，能够降低计算成本并摆脱对函数形式预设的依赖。

Abstract: In this paper, we consider the automated discovery of iterative optimization algorithms. We formulate the algorithm discovery task as a discrete optimization problem and search for new update functions using latent space Bayesian Optimization. The proposed framework first learns a continuous representation of the discrete space of update functions using variational autoencoders, transforming the algorithm discovery task from a discrete to a continuous search problem. The continuous representation is subsequently used to search for new algorithms using Bayesian optimization. Application to two case studies shows that the proposed approach can discover new update functions in symbolic form without any assumptions on the functional form of the update function. Moreover, the computational time required to discover the new update functions is lower than existing mathematical programming-based approaches.
